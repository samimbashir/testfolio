Selected Research Projects

01 MS Thesis · Proteogenomics

Evaluation of Proteogenomic Approaches for Neuropeptide Identification in Rattus Norvegicus Brain

Addressing the challenge of identifying low-abundance neuropeptides in highly complex neural tissue using tandem mass spectrometry and custom proteogenomic databases.

Biological Gap

Neuropeptides act as neuromodulators in the CNS, yet their systematic identification from brain proteomes remains limited by database incompleteness and spectral false-discovery rates. Standard proteomics pipelines miss peptidomic signals entirely.

The Pipeline

Python Linux/Bash MS/MS Search NeuroPep DB Proteogenomic DB FDR Analysis Custom DB Design

Constructed tiered proteogenomic databases of varying sizes; searched MS/MS spectra against known neuropeptide databases and genome-translated sequences. Automated the entire pipeline using Python and Bash scripting for reproducible execution.

Key Insight

Database size is a critical determinant of neuropeptide identification sensitivity and specificity. Larger, less-curated databases inflate false-discovery rates, while targeted peptidomic databases recover significantly more biologically relevant neuropeptide hits — a finding with direct implications for clinical neuroproteomics.

02 Cancer Informatics · Gene Expression

In Silico Gene Expression Analysis in Hepatocellular Carcinoma (HCC) and Chronic Hepatitis C (CHC) Across Age and Gender

A multi-variable transcriptomic study revealing how patient demographics modulate liver cancer gene expression — with implications for precision oncology stratification.

Biological Gap

Hepatocellular carcinoma exhibits striking heterogeneity across patient demographics, yet most expression datasets analyze cohorts in aggregate, masking clinically actionable age- and sex-specific gene regulatory patterns.

The Pipeline

R / Bioconductor GEO Datasets DESeq2 limma GSEA ggplot2 Heatmaps

Retrieved publicly available GEO expression data; performed differential expression analysis stratified by age group and gender using limma/DESeq2 in Bioconductor; visualized with volcano plots and heatmaps; pathway enrichment via GSEA.

Key Insight

Age- and gender-stratified analyses uncovered distinct transcriptomic signatures in HCC vs. CHC patients, with several immune-modulatory pathways showing differential enrichment in older female cohorts — pointing toward sex-hormone-mediated hepatoprotective mechanisms that are lost in carcinogenesis.

03 Proteomics · MS Data Analysis

Manual Validation Framework for Mass Spectrometry Proteomics Data: Designing a Reproducible Analysis Approach

Developing a structured, step-by-step framework for validating MS/MS peptide identifications that reduces researcher bias and increases inter-lab reproducibility.

Biological Gap

Automated MS/MS database searches produce large numbers of peptide-spectrum matches (PSMs) that require expert manual validation — but without a standardized framework, this process introduces subjective bias and limits reproducibility across labs and researchers.

The Pipeline

Python Mascot / Sequest Spectrum Annotation Ion Series Analysis Score Thresholding Manual PSM Review

Designed a decision-tree validation protocol examining b/y ion coverage, precursor mass accuracy, and spectral quality scores; applied across diverse peptide classes to benchmark automated search outputs.

Key Insight

A systematic, criterion-based manual validation approach recovers approximately 15–20% additional high-confidence PSMs that automated thresholding alone discards, while simultaneously flagging ambiguous spectra that automated pipelines incorrectly accept — demonstrating that structured human oversight meaningfully complements algorithmic scoring.
